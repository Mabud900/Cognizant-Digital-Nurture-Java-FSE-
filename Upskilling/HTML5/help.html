How to Register for Events

  1. Go to the Events section on the home page.
  2. Fill in your name, email, preferred date, and event type.
  3. Click Register Now to confirm your spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is registration free?
A: Most community events are free for residents.

Q: How do I cancel?
A: Email us at council@city.gov

Q: Can I register multiple family members?
A: Yes, submit a separate form for each person.
